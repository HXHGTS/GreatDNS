## Debian安装源-腾讯云

### Debian 7

```
rm -f /etc/apt/*.list
curl -sSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/HXHGTS/GreatDNS/master/source/debian/tencent/debian7_tencent_sources.list > /etc/apt/sources.list
apt update
```
